Despre Beagle

The beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling

Curiozitati:

  • Alongside the Bloodhound and Basset Hound, the beagle has one of the best developed senses of smell of any dog.[31] In the 1950s, John Paul Scott and John Fuller began a 13-year study of canine behavior. As part of this research, they tested the scenting abilities of various breeds by putting a mouse in a one-acre field and timing how long it took the dogs to find it. The beagles found it in less than a minute while Fox Terriers took 15 minutes and Scottish Terriers failed to find it at all.[31] The long ears and large lips of the beagle probably assist in trapping the scents close to the nose.
